A Timeline of the Mark Twain Title Company

Mark Twain Title Company traces our origins back to 1898, when we were known as Marion County Abstract Company, a name we still hold the rights to. The company has been through many changes over the years, but the one thing that hasn't changed is our dedication to providing the area with the best title insurance and real estate services around. Please take a loot at our company timeline, spanning over a century.
